# 💫 About Me:
I'm a Computer Science undergraduate at Tanta University (Class of 2027) with a dual passion for cybersecurity and front-end development. I believe the best builders are the ones who understand how things break — and that mindset drives everything I do.
On the security side, I hunt for vulnerabilities in web applications, targeting real-world threats like XSS, SQLi, and IDOR. I've responsibly disclosed critical bugs through bug bounty programs and earned recognition for findings that led to tangible security improvements. I've also trained at the National Telecommunication Institute (NTI) twice — once in cybersecurity fundamentals and most recently in Fortinet enterprise security, where I worked hands-on with FortiGate firewalls, VPNs, and intrusion prevention systems.
On the development side, I built responsive, component-based web applications as a React.JS Developer at the Digital Egypt Pioneers Initiative (DEPI), integrating RESTful APIs and applying modern JavaScript best practices.
I'm currently seeking opportunities in penetration testing or front-end development where I can contribute, grow, and keep breaking things — responsibly.
